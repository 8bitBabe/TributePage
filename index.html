Some highlights on Impa's life

  • April 13, 2017 - Impa is found in the University of Puerto Rico at Mayaguez and is adopted.
  • May 9, 2017 - Midna, her sister, is seen calmly standing next to her despite not getting along too well yet.
  • June 10, 2017 - Impa's first moving day!
  • July 22, 2017 - Impa and Midna are notably getting along better
  • September 20, 2017 - Hurricane Maria hits. Luckily, everyone was okay.
  • October 8, 2017 - Paw prints are taken for future tattoo.
  • November 12, 2017 - Tattoo of Midna and Impa's paws is now on furmom's upper back.
  • Decemeber 2017 - Impa's first Holidays. Knocks over Christmas tree only once.
  • January 2018 - Impa, Midna, furmom and furdad spend their first holidays together. Furdad starts his first professional job.
  • February 2018 - Impa's first birthday.
  • March 2018 - Impa's second moving event!
  • September 2018 - Impa's first cat tower. Claims top tier as her own.
  • December 13, 2018 - Impa gets a new little brother, Blue. He likes to pick on her and Midna a lot even though he's a few months old only. Doesn't knock over tree this year but comes very close to doing so.
  • February 2019 - Impa's second birthday!
  • May 13, 2019 - Impa catches a bug but recovers a week later thanks to a lot of love and care. Furmom finishes their Master's degree.
  • June 16, 2019 - First documented picture of all three cats on the cat tree.
  • June 20, 2019 - Impa crosses the Rainbow Bridge after not being able to recover from an asthma attack and, as the more humane option, was euthanized. She passed quietly in the arms of her furparents. She is terribly missed. May this page be a testament to how much she is, was and forever will be loved.
